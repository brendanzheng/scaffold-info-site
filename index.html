Frequently asked questions

Why is your website called Scaffold?

Scaffold is a reference to the practice of scaffolding, a technique whereby individuals engage with new knowledge while receiving external support.

What is the purpose of Scaffold?

Scaffold is a digital support designed to help users develop their thoughts in academic and real-world contexts.

Will Scaffold ever give you the answer?

No. Scaffold is designed to continually ask questions. It will not respond to users with declarative statements.

Does Scaffold make mistakes?

Scaffold draws exclusively from the context that the user provides and does not pull information from outside sources. If the user is interacting with Scaffold in a context where accuracy matters, they should independently consult and verify information.

Why is Scaffold green?

Brendan and Hillary were born and raised in New York City, where the default color for scaffolding is dark green.

Why does Scaffold move up instead of down?

The flow of the website is meant to stimulate the building up of ideas.
