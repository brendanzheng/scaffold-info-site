How to get the most out of it

Bring the context

Scaffold reasons only within your closed universe. The more of the relevant context you bring, the richer the questioning gets.

Sit with the questions

When Scaffold asks something hard, resist the urge to move on. The pause is where the thinking happens.

Capture as you go

Use sticky notes to catch the ideas that surface mid-interaction, then distill your takeaways at the end.

Verify the facts elsewhere

Scaffold can't check the accuracy of your statements. For factual answers, consult additional resources.
