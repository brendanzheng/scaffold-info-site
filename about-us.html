About us

A passion project, a long time in the making.

We've been building Scaffold for a while now — and thinking about it for even longer.

Why we built it

We kept noticing the same thing: the tools around us are racing to think for us. Ask a question, get an answer — the faster the better. But somewhere in that convenience, the muscle of thinking for yourself gets a little weaker every time.

We wanted the opposite. A quiet, dedicated space that takes your thinking seriously enough to not do it for you. Something that asks the next good question instead of handing over a conclusion.

Scaffold will not try to solve your problems, resolve your internal conflicts, or do your homework. Only you can do that. Scaffold will simply support you along the process, encouraging you to keep going.

Who we are

We're Brendan and Hillary — two people who care a lot about how people think, and who wanted to make something that honored that. Scaffold is our passion project, shaped over countless conversations about curiosity, reasoning, and what a genuinely useful thinking partner should feel like.

It's still growing, and we're building it in the open with the people testing it. If you're here, you're part of that.

What we believe

Good thinking is slow, effortful, and worth it. The point isn't to arrive at an answer as quickly as possible — it's to understand your own reasoning well enough to trust it. Scaffold is our attempt to make room for exactly that.

— Brendan & Hillary <3
